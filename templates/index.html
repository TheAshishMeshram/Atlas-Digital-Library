A sanctuary of knowledge, science, and human ingenuity.

Library Policies

Stewardship guidelines for a balanced academic community.

Borrowing Limit

Students can borrow up to three (3) books at any given time. Active issues are tracked under the personal shelf drawer.

Loan Duration

Standard student checkout duration is seven (7) calendar days. Extended renewals are subject to reservation queues.

Renewal Requests

Books can be renewed once if no active reservation exists from other scholars. Request renewal prior to due date.

Overdue Penalties

Failure to return materials results in temporary borrowing suspensions. Alerts are sent directly to student email accounts.
